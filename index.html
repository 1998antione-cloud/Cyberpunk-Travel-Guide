About Neon Prime

Neon Prime is a floating metropolis in the sky, known for its radiant skylines, cybernetic markets, and 24/7 nightlife. Founded in 2124 after the rise of the Sky Nations, the city blends advanced AI governance with old-school punk energy. Whether you're here to sightsee, party, or integrate, Neon Prime welcomes you.
